# ITEM #200 – Knowing–Doing Co-Progress Intelligence (KD-CPI)

File: ITEM-200-LITE.md

## One-Paragraph Summary

KD-CPI defines an intelligence paradigm in which knowing and acting evolve simultaneously under uncertainty, competition, and partial observability. It rejects the assumption that complete understanding must precede action.

## Core Idea

Knowledge and action co-evolve

Decisions occur under fuzzy metrics

Designed for real-world environments

## Why It Matters

Researchers: extends classical rational models

Engineers: supports adaptive systems

AI design: suitable for adversarial domains
