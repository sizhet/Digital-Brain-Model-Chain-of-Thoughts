# Citation Guide — DBM-COT

Digital Brain Model – Chain of Thought (DBM-COT)

This document explains how to cite DBM-COT releases in academic,
technical, and research contexts.

DBM-COT is published as a sequence of **DOI-anchored releases**.
Each release is a **frozen structural snapshot** of the evolving
DBM Structural Intelligence research corpus.

---

## Citation Philosophy

DBM-COT follows a **research-artifact release model**, where:

- The GitHub repository continues to evolve
- DOI releases remain immutable
- Each release corresponds to a stable research corpus

This ensures:

- scholarly traceability
- reproducibility
- algorithmic provenance
- version-specific attribution

## Project Context

DBM-COT is part of the broader Digital Brain Model (DBM) ecosystem:

- DBM-COT — Chain of Thought (research corpus)
- DBM-COA — Chain of Algorithms (implementation layer)
- DBM-COS — Chain of Solutions (application frameworks)

Together, these tracks explore **Structural Intelligence** as a
complementary AI paradigm alongside symbolic reasoning and neural models.
